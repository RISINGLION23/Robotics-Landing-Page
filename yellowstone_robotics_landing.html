What Covo can do

Built around your everyday life

From morning coffee to late-night snack runs, Covo handles the small stuff so you can focus on what matters.

Voice commands

Talk naturally. Covo understands full sentences — "bring me my glasses from the bedroom" just works, no app required.

Routine learning

Covo observes and remembers. After a week, it anticipates — having your morning items ready before you even ask.

Carry & deliver

With a 5kg payload and stable tray design, Covo hauls groceries, laundry, snacks, and more room to room.

Smart scheduling

Syncs with your calendar. Covo reminds you of meetings, medication times, or whenever the laundry is done.

Home mapping

SLAM navigation technology maps your home in minutes. Covo avoids pets, kids, and furniture without missing a beat.

Expandable modules

Clip on the air quality sensor, mini speaker, or charging pad. Covo grows with your needs over time.
